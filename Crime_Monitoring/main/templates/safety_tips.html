Your Safety Matters

1. Stay Alert

Be aware of your surroundings at all times, especially in unfamiliar or poorly lit areas.

2. Use Verified Transport

Always use trusted and verified transport options, especially when traveling alone.

3. Emergency Contacts

Keep emergency numbers saved and easily accessible on your phone and wallet.

4. Report Suspicious Activity

Don't hesitate to report anything unusual or suspicious using our crime report section.

5. Stay Connected

Share your live location with trusted friends or family when traveling alone at night.

6. Be Smart Online

Never share personal or sensitive data with unverified websites or unknown contacts.
